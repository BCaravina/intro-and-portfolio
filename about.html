About me

Front-end developer from São Paulo, Brasil

I was born and raised in Brazil, and my curiosity has always driven me to explore new ideas and opportunities. I earned a bachelor’s degree in Kinesiology and a master’s in Sports Performance, which led me to live in the United States for nearly 9 years. However, in pursuit of a more fulfilling path, I decided to switch careers and transition into tech last year.
I believe in the power of pursuing happiness, connecting with people, aiming for personal growth, and exploring the world. I’m excited about the future and about further developing my skills in technology. My goal is to use my skills to make a positive impact and change lives in meaningful ways.
